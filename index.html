Privacy Policy — Wall Flip Rush

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This policy describes how the mobile application Wall Flip Rush (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is operated by Wayne Rayner (“we”, “us”). Questions: waynerayner1@outlook.com.

1. Accounts

The App does not use user accounts. You do not register a username or password with us to play or to make purchases.

2. Game data on your device

The App stores game progress and settings locally on your device (for example: high score, in-game coins, cosmetics, mute preferences, and purchase entitlement flags). You can remove this data by uninstalling the App or clearing app storage in Android settings.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. Google may use identifiers and device information to show and measure ads, including in support of personalized advertising where allowed by law and your device settings. How Google uses data in connection with advertising is described in Google’s policies, including the Google Privacy Policy and resources for AdMob.

You can limit ad personalization on your device through your Google account and Android settings (for example, “Ads” or advertising preferences). Features such as Google’s UMP (user messaging platform) may be used where required to obtain consent for ads in certain regions.

4. In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

Purchases inside the App are processed by Google Play using Google Play Billing. Payment information is handled by Google according to the Google Payments Privacy Notice and related terms. We receive information needed to confirm what you bought (for example, product type and whether a purchase is active) so we can unlock content in the App—not your full card number.

5. What we do not operate

We do not run our own login system, social network, or game servers for core gameplay as described above. Data processed by Google (ads, payments, Play services) is subject to Google’s policies and your settings with Google.

6. Children

If the App is directed to children or included in Google Play’s “Designed for Families” program, additional rules apply for ads and data—follow Google Play and AdMob requirements for child-directed apps. Parents or guardians may contact us at the email above with privacy concerns.
